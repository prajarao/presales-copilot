FAQ

Common questions

Does it use the public internet to generate answers?
No. Every answer is drafted exclusively from the knowledge sources you upload — your own security policies, prior answers, FAQs, and docs. The AI never searches the web or uses external content for customer-facing answers. This is a hard guardrail, not a setting.
What is the free trial?
You get one complete AI-drafted questionnaire response for free — no credit card needed. We pre-load a sample knowledge base so you can see the quality of answers immediately, even before adding your own content. After your free draft, you'll be prompted to upgrade.
How long does it take to set up?
Most teams are drafting their first questionnaire within 15 minutes of signing up. The initial knowledge base setup (uploading your key security and product docs) takes 20–30 minutes. After that, each new questionnaire takes 2–3 minutes to set up before the AI starts drafting.
Who reviews and approves the answers before they go to customers?
Always a human. The AI produces a first draft — your team reviews, edits, and approves every answer before it can be exported. Low-confidence answers (flagged in amber or red) cannot be exported without explicit approval. The system is designed to support your reviewers, not replace them.
What happens to answers we approve?
Approved answers are automatically saved to your reusable answer library. Future questionnaires with similar questions will pull from this library first — so accuracy and confidence scores improve over time as your team approves more answers.
How is this different from Responsive.io or RFPIO?
Responsive and RFPIO are built for large enterprise bid teams with complex workflows and $800–1,200/seat price points. PreSales Copilot is built for smaller, faster pre-sales and SE teams who need a tool that works immediately without a 3-month implementation. It's AI-native, half the price, and designed around the SE workflow — not the procurement workflow.
